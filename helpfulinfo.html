Helpful Info

Sometimes those who need help don't seek it because:

  • they think they are alone
  • they think that their problem aren't important enough for people to listen. They were taught to not talk about how they feel.
  • they are afraid of judgements. People may not believe in mental disorder and try to use it against you.
  • they simply don't know where to look for help

Those helplines exists for a reason for emergency purposes to just wanting to talk to someone or even saving a life. They are also free or relatively at cheap rate.
